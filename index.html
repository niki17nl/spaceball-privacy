Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Nikita Lesnikov ("we", "our", "us") handles information in connection with the mobile game Multi Ball: Tap & Evolve ("the App"), available on Google Play and the Apple App Store. By installing or using the App you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

The App itself does not ask you to register, log in, or provide any personal information directly. However, certain third-party services integrated into the App may automatically collect limited data as described below.

1.1 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

  • Game progress, high scores, unlocked skins, and in-game settings are stored locally on your device using the platform's standard storage APIs.
  • This data is not transmitted to any server controlled by us.
  • You can delete this data at any time by uninstalling the App.

1.2 Data Collected by Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party services, which may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob — serves advertisements and may collect a device identifier (Advertising ID on Android, IDFA on iOS), IP address, approximate location derived from IP, and basic device and ad-interaction information for purposes of ad delivery, measurement, and fraud prevention.
  • Google Play Billing (Android) and Apple In-App Purchase (iOS) — process in-app purchases. Payment details are handled entirely by Google or Apple. We never see or store your payment information.
  • Google Play Games Services (Android) and Apple Game Center (iOS) — handle leaderboards and achievements. If you sign in with your Google or Apple account, your gamer profile, scores, and achievements may be shared with the respective service according to their terms. Sign-in is optional.
  • Google Play Services / Apple System Services — required platform components used to run the services above.

2. How We Use the Information

The limited data collected by third-party services is used to:

  • Display advertisements (including personalized ads where permitted by you and applicable law).
  • Process and verify in-app purchases.
  • Save and display your scores on global leaderboards (only if you opt in to Google Play Games or Game Center).
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and technical issues.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We ourselves do not build advertising profiles, sell your data, or use it for purposes other than operating the App.

3. Advertising

The App displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may use your device's Advertising ID (Android) or IDFA (iOS) and other limited information to deliver more relevant ads.

You can control ad personalization at any time through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → "Opt out of Ads Personalization" (or "Delete advertising ID" on Android 12+).
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → toggle off "Allow Apps to Request to Track". You can also decline the App Tracking Transparency prompt the first time the App requests it.

For more information, see the Google Ads Privacy Policy and the AdMob Help Center.

4. In-App Purchases

The App offers optional in-app purchases (for example, cosmetic skin packs) processed exclusively through Google Play Billing on Android or Apple In-App Purchase on iOS. We do not collect, store, or have access to your credit card, bank account, or any other payment information. All purchases are subject to the Google Play Terms of Service or the Apple Media Services Terms.

5. Leaderboards and Achievements

If you choose to sign in with Google Play Games (Android) or Apple Game Center (iOS), your public gamer profile (gamer name, avatar) and your in-game scores and achievements may be uploaded to Google's or Apple's servers and displayed on public leaderboards. This is an optional feature.

  • To revoke Google Play Games access: myaccount.google.com/permissions.
  • To manage Game Center: iOS Settings → [your name] → Game Center.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or under 16 in the European Economic Area, or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and we will take reasonable steps to delete it.

7. Your Rights (GDPR / UK GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access the data we hold about you.
  • The right to request correction or deletion of your data.
  • The right to restrict or object to processing.
  • The right to data portability.
  • The right to withdraw consent at any time.
  • The right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Because we do not collect personal data ourselves, most data requests should be directed to the respective third-party service (Google or Apple). For requests concerning the App itself, contact us at the address below.

8. Your Rights (CCPA — California Residents)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to request deletion, and the right not to be discriminated against for exercising your rights. We do not sell personal information.

9. Data Retention

Data stored locally on your device remains until you delete it or uninstall the App. Data handled by third-party services is retained according to their respective retention policies.

10. Permissions Used by the App

Android

  • INTERNET — required to load advertisements and process purchases.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — required to detect whether the device is online before making network requests.
  • com.google.android.gms.permission.AD_ID — required by Google AdMob on Android 13 and above to access the Advertising ID.

iOS

  • App Tracking Transparency (ATT) — on iOS 14.5 and above, the App will ask for your permission before allowing AdMob to access the IDFA for cross-app tracking. You may decline at any time.
  • Network access — required to load advertisements and process purchases.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always indicate when the policy was most recently revised. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
